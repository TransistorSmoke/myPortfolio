About Me

BACKGROUND

My name is Jason and I am a hardware design engineer. But I still have this passion in programming, graphics design and web development. As a hobby, I used to design banners, posters and other graphics for family and friends when I was still an engineering student. And from there, I tried delving into web development.

EXPERIENCE

I got my first job as a software engineer back in 2009. I learned to create applications with Sybase PowerBuilder (Powerscript), manage databases (MS SQL Server) and received training in ASP.NET. But, I left in 2011 to try opportunities in hardware design.

From 2011 until 2013, even while working as a hardware engineer, I worked as a freelance web and graphics designer. I really got to use and hone my design skills through actual practice.

I stopped being a freelancer by the next half of 2013. Instead, I volunteered to help family members, friends and some of my clients from my freelancing days, on matters related to graphics and web design. From time to time, I would receive request for assistance from them and I was always glad to help, as I loved doing the tasks and I continuously learned that way.

NEXT STEP

Finally, after some time with electronic hardware engineering, I realize that it's time to go back to these real interests of mine. I am now taking online courses in web development and programming (HTML5/CSS3/Javascript, Node.JS, ReactJS, AngularJS and Python) and practicing what I learned in order to refresh old skills and acquire new ones to get back on track.
